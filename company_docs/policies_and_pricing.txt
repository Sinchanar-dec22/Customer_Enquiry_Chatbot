REMOTE WORKING POLICY
====================

What is Remote Working?
Remote working allows eligible employees to work from home or approved locations while maintaining full productivity and security standards.

Who Can Work Remotely?
- Full-time employees after 6 months of employment
- Approved by direct manager and HR department
- Some roles may have restrictions due to client requirements
- Contract and part-time employees: Case-by-case basis

Remote Work Requirements
- Must use company VPN at all times (no exceptions)
- Multi-factor authentication mandatory for all accounts
- Secure, private workspace required (quiet environment)
- Minimum internet connectivity: 10 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload
- Company-approved devices only (no personal laptops for work)
- Updated antivirus and firewall software required
- Never download company data to personal devices

Work Schedule While Remote
- Core work hours: 9 AM to 6 PM IST (Monday-Friday)
- Flexible start times within core hours (with manager approval)
- 1-hour lunch break (not deducted from work hours)
- Must be available for team meetings and stand-ups
- Daily stand-up at 10:00 AM IST is mandatory
- Weekly team meetings participation required
- Response time: 30 minutes during work hours

Security Requirements
- All work communication through company-approved tools only
- Slack/Teams for chat; Email for formal communication
- Never send sensitive data via personal email
- Use encrypted messaging for confidential information
- VPN disconnection results in immediate session end
- Password-protect all devices
- Lock computer when stepping away (even briefly)
- Never access company systems from public WiFi
- All devices subject to monthly security audits

Office Requirements
- Minimum 2 days per week in office required
- First and third Friday are mandatory office days
- Ad-hoc in-office meetings allowed with 24-hour notice
- Quarterly team building sessions in office (mandatory)
- Client meetings may require in-office presence

Equipment & Reimbursements
- Company provides: Laptop, VPN token, basic peripherals
- Employee can request:
  - Monitor reimbursement: Up to ₹5,000
  - Keyboard/mouse: Up to ₹2,000
  - Desk/chair: Up to ₹10,000 (one-time)
  - Internet reimbursement: ₹500/month
- All equipment must be returned upon separation
- Damage charges apply for negligence

Video Conference Requirements
- Camera MUST be on during all client-facing meetings
- Professional background required
- Blur background or use company virtual backdrop
- Proper lighting and audio quality essential
- Test audio/video 5 minutes before meetings
- Mute when not speaking in group calls

Confidentiality & Data Security
- Never discuss company matters in public spaces
- Family members should not be in background during calls
- No sharing of company documents on personal devices
- Data must be deleted from personal devices after work
- Breach of confidentiality: Subject to disciplinary action

Monitoring & Compliance
- Company may monitor VPN usage and activity logs
- Desktop activity monitoring may be enabled
- Email monitoring follows company policy
- Regular security compliance checks conducted
- Quarterly remote device security audits mandatory
- Any security violations reported immediately

Violations & Consequences
- Unauthorized personal device use: Written warning
- VPN disconnection during work hours: Disciplinary action
- Security breach due to negligence: Suspension/Termination
- Repeated non-compliance: Performance review impact
- Intentional policy violation: Immediate termination

Leave While Working Remotely
- Annual leave: Document hand-over required
- Sick leave: Notify manager by 9 AM on that day
- Set out-of-office message with contact person
- During leave, maintain emergency contact availability
- No access to VPN during approved leave

Equipment Troubleshooting
- Contact IT support: 24/7 availability
- Remote support available via TeamViewer/AnyDesk
- Hardware replacement within 24 hours (if critical)
- Internet issues: Use mobile hotspot as backup
- VPN troubleshooting: Contact IT immediately

Termination & Device Return
- Upon separation, return all company equipment
- 2-business-day deadline for device return
- Inventory check-out process required
- Data wipe performed by IT department
- Final reimbursements processed within 30 days
