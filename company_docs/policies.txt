TechNova Company Policies

1. Password Policy
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
- Password expires every 90 days

2. Remote Work Policy

Remote Work Eligibility
- Available after 6 months of employment
- Approved by direct manager and HR
- Roles with client-facing responsibilities may have restrictions
- Full-time employees preferred for remote work

Remote Work Requirements
- Employees must use company VPN at all times
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) mandatory on all accounts
- Secure, private workspace required
- Minimum internet speed: 10 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload
- Company-approved devices only (no personal devices for work)
- Antivirus and firewall software must be enabled

Work Hours & Availability
- Core work hours: 9 AM – 6 PM IST (Monday to Friday)
- Must be available during team meetings and stand-ups
- Flexible start/end times within core hours, with manager approval
- Lunch break: 1 hour (not deducted from work hours)

Communication & Meetings
- Daily stand-ups via video conference (10:00 AM IST)
- Weekly team meetings mandatory
- All client meetings require video camera ON
- Response time expectation: 30 minutes during work hours
- Use company Slack/Teams for all work communication

Security & Compliance
- No unauthorized sharing of company data
- Encrypted messaging for sensitive information
- Monthly security compliance audits
- Remote device security scans quarterly
- VPN connection mandatory for accessing company systems
- Never leave unlocked devices unattended

Work-from-Office Requirements
- Minimum 2 days per week in office (or as per project needs)
- First and third Friday mandatory office days
- Ad-hoc in-office meetings with 24-hour notice
- Quarterly team-building sessions in office

Leave While Remote Working
- Annual leave: Work must be handed over before leave
- Sick leave: Notification required by 9 AM
- Out-of-office message with contact person mandatory
- Emergency contact always active

Equipment & Support
- Company provides laptop and VPN hardware token
- Monitor/peripherals reimbursable (₹5000 limit)
- Internet reimbursement: ₹500/month
- Technical support available 24/7
- Equipment must be returned on separation

Violations & Consequences
- Unauthorized use of personal devices: Warning
- VPN disconnection during work hours: Disciplinary action
- Security breach due to negligence: Suspension/Termination
- Repeated non-compliance: Performance review impact

3. Leave Policy
- Casual Leave: 12 days/year
- Sick Leave: 10 days/year
- Paid Leave: 18 days/year

4. Data Protection Policy
- Customer data encrypted using AES-256
- Unauthorized sharing prohibited
- Quarterly security audits conducted

5. Device Usage Policy
- Only company-approved software allowed
- External USB devices restricted
- Antivirus mandatory

6. Incident Reporting Policy
- Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour
- Escalation matrix followed for critical incidents

7. Refund Policy
- Refund requests accepted within 7 days
- Refunds processed within 5 business days

8. Account Suspension Policy
Accounts may be suspended for:
- Payment failures
- Security violations
- Fraudulent activity
